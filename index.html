About

Dr. Dhirendra Paudel knows there is no single technique that works for everybody, so he maintains to discipline himself on emerging therapies, psychopharmacological treatments, and psychological trends to provide the most comprehensive program that works for each of his client’s individual needs. He has experience in psychopharmacology, Cognitive Behavior Therapy, Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy, and many others.

Dr. Dhirendra Paudel received his education at the Southern Medical University with a Master of Medicine in Psychiatry and Mental Health. He also holds a Bachelor in Medicine and Bachelor in Surgery from Tribhuvan University. He is an avid researcher, and his works have been published in various journals. His research interests include psychotherapeutic interventions (esp. cognitive behavior therapy and rational emotive behavior therapy), psychopharmacology, and sleep medicine.
